Schedule an online meeting with more than 250 people

You can schedule a meeting with more than 250 people by using the Microsoft Office Live Meeting service.

You may also want to consult the Over 250 Limit Template, which provides guidelines for adding Microsoft Lync Server 2010 dial-in conferencing to a meeting that uses Live Meeting service. The specifics will vary greatly depending on your network, so the document is offered as a general set of guidelines. For details, see Adding Lync Server 2010 Dial-in Conferencing to a Live Meeting Service Meeting (coming soon).
